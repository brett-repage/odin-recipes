Caprese Avocado Toast

Description

Indulge in a vibrant and wholesome breakfast or snack with our Caprese Avocado Toast. Creamy mashed avocado is spread over toasted whole grain bread and topped with ripe tomato slices, fresh mozzarella, and fragrant basil leaves. Finished with a drizzle of balsamic glaze, this toast is a delightful combination of flavors and textures that will leave you satisfied and energized.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 large tomato, thinly sliced
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil Leaves
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Toast the slices of whole grain bread until golden brown and crispy.
  2. While the bread is toasting, halve the avocado, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh into a small bowl. Mash the avocado with a fork and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Once the bread is toasted, spread the mashed avocado evenly onto each slice.
  4. Top the avocado toast with slices of tomato, fresh mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves.
  5. Drizzle with balsamic glaze.
  6. Serve the Caprese Avocado Toast immediately, and enjoy the fresh and flavorful combination of avocado, tomato, and mozzarella!
